Why Open Data Designs

What makes us different.

Three principles that define every engagement — and separate ODD from every other data consultancy.

01

Architecture-Led on Every Engagement

Darwin Fisk leads architecture, strategy, and standards on every project. Engineering delivery is executed by a skilled team under his direct oversight — so you get principal-level thinking applied consistently, at scale.

02

Enablement Over Dependency

We build solutions your team can understand, manage, and grow without us. That includes growing your people — we have trained report analysts into data engineers and good writers into technical practitioners. The goal is your capability, not your reliance on a consultant.

03

A Practice Built to Scale With You

ODD is not a solo practice. We bring the right resources — internal team, trained specialists, and delivery partners — to match the scope of your engagement. Early-phase assessments are nimble; full platform builds have the depth to match.

Client Outcomes

Real results from real engagements.

Every engagement is designed to produce outcomes you can measure — in time saved, costs reduced, and decisions made with confidence.

Reporting Cycles: Weeks → Hours
